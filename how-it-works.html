How RakshaX Works

RakshaX is designed for real emergencies — fast, simple, and independent of a mobile phone.

Step 1: SOS Trigger

In an emergency, the user presses the SOS button on the RakshaX device. Different devices support different press patterns (long press, double press, or custom pattern).

Step 2: Live Location Captured

The device immediately captures live GPS location using its inbuilt module and sends it securely through the network.

Step 3: Alerts Sent

SOS alerts are sent simultaneously to:

  • Emergency contacts
  • RakshaX control system
  • Nearby verified women volunteers

Step 4: Community Response

Nearby volunteers receive the alert and one volunteer accepts the request. Others are automatically hidden to avoid crowding or confusion.

Step 5: Help Reaches Faster

The responding volunteer navigates to the user, while the system continues tracking and logging events for safety and accountability.

Future Integrations

  • Police dashboard integration
  • Emergency calling with data sharing
  • Audio & video evidence capture
  • Psychologist support access

Why This System Works

RakshaX reduces reaction time, removes panic decisions, and brings help from nearby real people — not just notifications.
