Know Your Volunteers

RakshaX works with verified women volunteers who are ready to help during emergencies — safely, responsibly, and with respect for privacy.

Who Are RakshaX Volunteers?

RakshaX volunteers are verified women from nearby communities who choose to assist during SOS situations. They are not random users — each volunteer goes through a structured verification and approval process.

Volunteer Verification Process

Identity Verification
Background Review
Manual Approval by Admin
Code of Conduct Acceptance

Only after completing all steps does a volunteer become eligible to receive SOS alerts.

What Users Can See

  • First name & last initial (e.g. Anjali S.)
  • Verified status badge
  • Age range (not exact age)
  • Number of successful responses
  • Approximate area (not live location)

What Is Never Shared

  • Phone numbers or personal contact details
  • Exact home or work addresses
  • ID documents
  • Real-time location (unless SOS is active)

Location-Based Visibility

Users may browse volunteer availability by city or area name. This view is informational only and does not allow tracking, contacting, or monitoring volunteers.

Safety & Accountability

  • All volunteer actions are logged
  • Misuse results in immediate removal
  • Emergency communication is masked
  • Community-first, authority-assisted model

Trust is Built Through Transparency

RakshaX is committed to safe, responsible community support.
