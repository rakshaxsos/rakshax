The RakshaX Safety Device

A women-first SOS device ecosystem designed to work even without a mobile phone.

One System. Multiple Forms.

RakshaX is not limited to a single gadget. It is designed to exist in forms that naturally stay with a woman — without changing her routine.

Available Device Forms

Wearable Band

Comfortable daily-wear band with discreet SOS trigger.

Pendant / Locket

Lightweight pendant designed to blend with personal accessories.

Shoe Sole

Hidden safety layer inside footwear with pressure-based SOS activation.

Mobile Cover

External SOS trigger integrated into everyday phone usage.

How the Device Works

  • Built-in SIM connectivity
  • Inbuilt GPS for live location
  • Works independently of mobile phone
  • Multiple SOS trigger patterns
  • Secure communication with RakshaX system

Designed for Real Emergencies

RakshaX devices are built keeping panic situations in mind. No screen, no unlocking, no searching — just a simple, reliable SOS action.

Planned Enhancements

  • Audio & video evidence recording
  • Automatic emergency calling
  • Police & authority integration
  • Smaller & optimized hardware versions

Important Note

RakshaX devices are currently under development and testing. Specifications and features may evolve as the system matures.
